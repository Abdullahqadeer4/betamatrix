About Us

Medical Billing historically has always been an opaque process. Too often, physicians are left uncomfortable about the true state of affairs of their billing and the status of their accounts receivable. Betametrix goes to extraordinary lengths to make transparent what for too long has remained opaque. Our clients receive data based granular accounting of their revenue cycle and glitches and bottlenecks in the revenue cycle are identified with clarity and precision.

Why Choose us?

Our company approach focuses on bringing a better user interface for our client’s business to meet the emerging business environments. We achieve this through our Three Phase Process

Why Choose us?

Every new account begins with a detailed audit of prior billing practices by the Betametrix auditing team. Then a dedicated group of expert billers are assigned to every account.

Why Choose us?

Too often, billing companies settle with merely harvesting “low hanging fruit”. Not at Betametrix: here, the emphasis in our company culture is to always be striving to harvest the “high hanging fruit”.
